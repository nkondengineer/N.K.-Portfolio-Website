About Me

Hello! My name is Nayan Kondakalla. I am a recent Biomedical Engineering graduate from Stony Brook University with a background in mechanical and molecular/cellular engineering. I enjoy finding ways to directly improve the health of patients and push the boundaries of medical technologies. I am constantly learning new skills and honing the ones that I have.

Recently, I have had the pleasure of interning at Stony Brook Hospital where I observed the many ways that healthcare providers work hard to provide top-quality care to their patients. Wanting to do my part, I prototyped a neonatal ECG monitoring system as my senior design project. Though I still have much to learn, I am ready to take any challenge head-on!
